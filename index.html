~ Onigiri Recipe ~

Simple Recipe to make!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups Japanese short-grain rice (sushi rice)
  • 2 ½ cups water (for cooking rice)
  • 1 tsp salt
  • Tuna + mayo

Optional fillings:

  • Tuna + mayo
  • Cooked salmon (flaked)
  • Pickled plum (umeboshi)
  • Seasoned seaweed
  • Nori (seaweed sheets), cut into strips or squares

Instructions

  1. Cook the Rice
    2.  Rinse the rice 2–3 times until the water runs clear.
    Cook it using a rice cooker or on the stovetop with
    2½ cups of water. Let it steam for 10 minutes after cooking.

  2. Prepare Fillings (optional)
    3.  While rice cooks, prep your filling (e.g., mix tuna with
    a bit of mayo or flake cooked salmon).

  3. Wet & Salt Your Hands
    4.  Fill a small bowl with water. Wet your hands and rub a pinch
    of salt between them. This keeps rice from sticking and adds flavor.

  4. Shape the Onigiri
    5.  Take a scoop of warm rice (~½ cup). Flatten it slightly in your hand,
    place a small amount of filling in the center. Fold the rice around the
    filling and shape it into a triangle (or ball if easier).

  5. Add Nori
    6.  Wrap a strip or small sheet of nori around the onigiri, like a handle.

  6. Serve or Pack
    7.  Eat immediately or wrap and store for a lunchbox (bento). Best enjoyed fresh!
